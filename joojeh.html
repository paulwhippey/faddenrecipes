Chicken Joojeh

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts, cubed
  • Thumb sized piece of fresh ginger, grated
  • 4 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 4 tsp turmeric
  • Greek yoghurt
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • Salt to taste
  • Freshly ground pepper
  • 1 or 2 onions, cut into wedges
  • 1 Red pepper
  • 1 Yellow Pepper
  • Pack of mushrooms
  • Basmati rice to serve
  • Salad to serve

    • Method

    1. 1st prepare the marinade by adding the yoghurt into a large bowl. Add the 4 tsp turmeric, crushed garlic, grated ginger, lemon juice to the yoghurt. Mix the ingredients together in the bowl. Add the pepper and salt to taste.

    2. Next, add the chicken chunks into the yoghurt and mix to coat the chicken. Cover with clingfilm and put in the fridge. It's best to leave it here for at least an hour, but overnight is recommended.

    3. Once marinaded, prepare the veg for the skewers. Cut the peppers into square chunks and cut the onions into wedges. Place the mushrooms, peppers and mushrooms onto skewers (2 per person is enough).

    4. Add the marinaded chicken to skewers (again 2 per person is enough).

    5. Cook the chicken and veg skewers in the oven at 180 degrees C, or even better, cook over a charcoal BBQ!

    6. Serve with basmati rice and salad.
