Mexican Bean Soup

Ingredients

  • One cal spray oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 red pepper, cut into chunks
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 2 tsp mild chilli powder
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 400g can black beans
  • 400g can butter beans
  • 100ml vegetable stock

    • Method

    1. Heat the oil in a medium pan, add the onion and pepper and fry, stirring frequently, for 10 mins.

    2. Stir in the garlic and spices, then tip in the tomatoes and beans with their liquid, half a can of water and the bouillon powder. Simmer, covered, for 15 mins.

    3. Season and then ladle the soup into two bowls
