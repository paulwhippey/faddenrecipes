Quorn Chilli

Ingredients

  • 2 Passata
  • 2 tins chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tin butter beans
  • 1 tin black eyed beans
  • 1 tin red kidney beans
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp ground coriander
  • Chilli powder to taste
  • 4 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 peppers, cubed
  • 1 pack of mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 red chillies, finely chopped
  • 150ml beef stock
  • 1 pack quorn mince
  • Salt to taste
  • Serve with basmati rice

    • Method

    1. Fry the onions in a large pot until they are slightly browned, be careful not to char them. Add the chillies and garlic, fry for a minute or two.

    2. Add the cumin and coriander and briefly fry until they become fragrant, stirring regularly. Add the passata and tinned tomatoes, add the chicken stock. Stir together. Add the butter beans, black eyed beans and red kidney beans. Stir together.

    3. Add the quorn mince. Then add the peppers and mushrooms. Simmer until it is reduced. Add the chilli powder and salt to taste. Serve with basmati rice, or for a low calorie version, serve with a pitta bread.
