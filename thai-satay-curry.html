Thai Satay Curry

For the Sauce:

  • 137 g dry roasted peanuts
  • Can of coconut milk
  • 300 ml chicken stock
  • 2 x red chillies roughly chopped
  • 1 x green chilli roughly chopped
  • 4 x garlic cloves roughly chopped
  • 3 cm fresh ginger roughly chopped
  • 3 tsp ground coriander
  • 3 tsp ground cumin
  • Fish sauce to taste

    • For the Curry:

  • 4 x chicken breasts
  • 2 x peppers
  • Small pack of mushrooms

    • Method

    1. Blend the sauce ingredients in a blender, add fish sauce and chillies to taste

    2. Brown off the chicken in a big saucepan

    3. Add sauce with the chicken, bring to a simmer and reduce

    4. In another saucepan, fry the peppers and mushrooms and wilt down

    5. Add peppers and mushrooms to the reduced curry sauce

    6. Serve with basmati rice
