Chilli, ginger and honey turkey chow mein

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 25g honey
  • 2 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • 2 tbsp tamari
  • 1 red chilli finely chopped
  • 4cm fresh ginger grated
  • 1 carrot sliced into matchsticks
  • 2 spring onions sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves sliced
  • 1 red pepper sliced
  • 100g brown rice noodles
  • 300g free-range turkey strips
  • 1 tbsp seaweed salad (optional)
  • 1 tsp black sesame seeds or nigella seeds (optional)

    • Method

    1. Make the dressing by mixing the honey, vinegar and tamari in a bowl. Set aside.

    2. Boil a kettle and half fill a medium saucepan with lightly salted boiling water. Bring to the boil and add the noodles. Gently separate the noodles with a fork and boil for 3 minutes. Drain and rinse well to prevent them sticking together.

    3. Heat a large wok with 1 tbsp sesame oil on a medium-high heat. Fry the turkey until turning golden brown.

    4. Add the pepper, garlic, ginger and chilli to the wok. Cook for 3 minutes, then add the carrot and toss through. Add the noodles, half the spring onions, half the seaweed, half the sesame seeds and the dressing. Mix well to combine and heat through. Check the turkey is cooked through.

    5. Once the turkey is cooked through, serve, garnished with the remaining spring onions, seaweed and sesame seeds.
