Leek and Potato Soup

Ingredients

  • One cal oil spray
  • 225g leeks, sliced
  • 225g potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 600ml vegetable stock
  • 100ml skimmed milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Method

    1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan and add the leeks, potatoes and garlic. Cook over a gentle heat for 2 - 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.

    2. Add the stock to the pan and bring to the boil. Reduce the heat, cover and simmer for 25 minutes.

    3. Carefully ladle half the soup into a food processor and blend until smooth. Return the blended soup to the pan and add the milk and seasoning. Heat through and serve.
