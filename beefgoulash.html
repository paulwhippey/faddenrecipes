Beef Goulash

Ingredients

  • 4 or 5 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1KG beef cubes
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 2 passata
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 3 tsp hot paprika
  • Chilli powder to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Method

    1. Brown the onions in a frying pan, be careful not to char them. Add the garlic to the onions and fry for a few minutes. Add to the slow cooker.

    2. Brown off the beef cubes in a frying pan. Add to the slow cooker.

    3. Add the chopped tomatoes, passata, hot paprika, smoked paprika to the slow cooker, stir to cover the ingredients.

    4. Cook for at least 8 hours in the slow cooker. Season to taste and add the chilli powder to taste. Serve with your choice of side!
