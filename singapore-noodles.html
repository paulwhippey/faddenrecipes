Singapore Style Noodles

Ingredients

  • 2 onions, 1 roughly chopped, 1 finely sliced
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 red chilli
  • 2.5 cm fresh ginger, peeled and roughly chopped
  • 4 tbsp medium curry powder
  • 1 tbsp turmeric
  • 1 tbsp chinese five spice powder
  • 1 tbsp thai fish sauce
  • 2 chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • 1 pack prawns, de-veined
  • 250g dried noodles
  • 2 carrots, cut into batons
  • 2 red peppers, deseeded and sliced
  • 150g sugar snap peas
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice wine vinegar

    • Method

    1. Put the roughly chopped onion, garlic, chilli, ginger, curry powder, turmeric, five spice powder and fish sauce into a food processor and blitz into a fine paste. Add water to get it to blend if needed.

    2. Put the chicken in a bowl and add one third of the spice paste. If using the prawns, marinate those separately. Coat well and leave to marinate for at least 5 minutes.

    3. Cook the noodles according to the pack instructions. Rinse in water and drain.

    4. Fry the chicken in a pan at a high heat, transfer to a plate and set aside.

    5. Reduce the heat to medium and the rest of the spice paste and cook for approximately 2 minutes. Add the carrots, peppers, sugar snaps, sliced onion and approximately 400ml boiling water (if needed). Cook for a few minutes, until softened.

    6. Return the chicken to the pan, add the noodles and stir together until hot.

    7. Add the prawns and cook until just done.

    8. Add the soy sauce and vinegar and serve in two bowls.
